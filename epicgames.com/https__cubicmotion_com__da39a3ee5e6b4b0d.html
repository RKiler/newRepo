Cubic Motion joins the Unreal Engine Team

Together, we'll push the boundaries of digital human technology

Cubic Motion’s management team is delighted to announce the company’s acquisition by Epic Games.

The combination unites the world’s most powerful game engine and the world-leading character creation technology of 3Lateral, with Cubic’s state-of-the-art vision and animation technology.

Together, we’ll push the boundaries of digital human technology, bringing ever more realism and immersive content to all forms of visual entertainment.

Our commitment to servicing the needs of the video game industry remains unchanged – we intend to remain the partner of choice for developers who need the most compelling digital characters.

Cubic Motion is particularly excited to bring Epic Games to Manchester, the birthplace of modern computing and once home to Alan Turing. The wealth of talent in the city and its many universities will be invaluable to Epic’s story as we continue to make breakthroughs in computing.

As we look forward with excitement to this new chapter in our story, and the story of digital entertainment, we’d like to thank all the staff who’ve worked so hard to get here, all our customer and partners, and our supportive investors NorthEdge Capital.
